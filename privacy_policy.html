Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1st, 2025

Welcome to OSakhi — a mobile app developed by an independent developer (“we,” “us,” “our”). OSakhi allows users to upload documents of any type and ask questions based on their content. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information and your rights.

1 Information We Collect

  • • Uploaded Documents: All documents you upload (PDFs, txt, etc.) are stored securely to power our AI-driven Q&A feature.
  • • Email Address: Collected if you opt to create an account or sign-in feature.
  • • Usage Data: Anonymous logs (e.g., session length, document type, feature usage) to improve performance.
  • • Device Data: Includes OS version, app version, device model, IP address, and crash telemetry for diagnosis.

2 How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • • Provide AI-powered document parsing and Q&A features.
  • • Improve our app via analytics and diagnostics.
  • • Communicate important updates (e.g., terms, maintenance).
  • • We never sell personal data or marketing information.

3 Data Storage & Security

All documents and data are encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Access is strictly limited to authorized personnel. We conduct routine security reviews.
